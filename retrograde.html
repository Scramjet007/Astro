Educational Note

In Vedic Astrology (Jyotish), a planet is Vakri (Crooked/Retrograde) when it appears to move backward in the zodiac.

This is an optical illusion caused when Earth (Prithvi), moving faster on the inner orbit, overtakes an outer planet like Mars or Jupiter.
