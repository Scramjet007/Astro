Planetary Ephemeris for March 2025

Legend:

This polar plot visualizes the movement traces of planets for the selected month. Each colored line represents a planet's path.

The radial distance from the center indicates the day of the month (1st closest, last day furthest). Aries starts at 0 degrees longitude (right side of the circle), and longitudes increase counter-clockwise. Concentric circles mark every 5th day for easier reference.

Hover over a planet's path to see the planet, the date, and the zodiac sign it is in.
