Meet Deepak Jain

Deepak Jain is an Aerospace Engineer, Educator, and lifelong seeker of the deeper truths of existence. He has been associated with some of India’s premier institutions, including IIT Kanpur and IIT Delhi, where he worked in areas such as flight testing of aircraft, flight simulation, and flight measurements. Over the years, he has taught undergraduate and postgraduate students in Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering, sharing both technical expertise and a passion for learning. His journey into Vedic and modern astrology reflects the same precision and analytical curiosity that define his engineering career. Deepak views astrology as a symbolic language of the universe — a way to understand patterns, possibilities, and pathways that empower individuals to make conscious choices. Blending scholarly rigor with the depth of India’s spiritual traditions, he creates tools, apps, and learning resources that make astrology accessible, practical, and meaningful in today’s world. Through this platform, he invites you to explore the cosmic order not as superstition, but as a profound system of guidance, insight, and clarity.
